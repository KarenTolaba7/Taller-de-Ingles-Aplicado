Verb Tenses from comic The Umbrella Academy

Verb Tense In The Umbrella Academy

Simple Present

It is a verb tense used to describe habitual actions that happen with a certain frequency.

Present Continuous

Actions of something that happens temporarily in the present

Present Perfect

Actions that occurred at a moment in the past but are still occurring in the present.

Simple Past

Verb tense used to talk about actions beginning and ending in the past

Simple Future

Verb tense used to talk about actions that will occur in the future.
